About The Flushing A

The Flushing A began as an A&W drive-in in the mid-1960s, founded by Larry & Chris Melancon. Larry had previously operated A&W locations in the Flint area before moving to Flushing in 1970, where he built a new dining location on Cherry Street and added a dining room in the early 1970s. The original Clio Road drive-in was part of their earlier operations.

Larry was widely respected for his strong work ethic, servant leadership, and commitment to mentoring staff. He was deeply involved in civic service and the Rotary, and many locals remember him as a generous, hands-on business owner. Larry passed in 2016, but his legacy continues in the community.

One of the Flushing A’s signature draws has always been its red sauce—a hot dog topping beloved by locals. The red sauce tradition traces back to Flint-area A&Ws, where a homemade ketchup alternative became a family recipe passed down for generations and has never been bottled for sale. Locals ask about purchasing it, but the only place to buy it is on a Flushing A hot dog.

Today, The Flushing A remains a family-owned place. A continuation their father’s vision - keeping the grill hot, the buns warm and soft, and the root beer flowing by the gallon (yes, you can buy a gallon)! Whether you’re here for a family dinner, a family pack of hot dogs and root beer - or just a sweet treat, The Flushing A is here in beautiful downtown Flushing to serve you.
