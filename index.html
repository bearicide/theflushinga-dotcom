about & ckc services

systems · reset · shine

CKC is for small operators who want restaurant-level standards without corporate nonsense: clearer systems, better tools, and a kitchen that doesn’t feel like a crime scene once the lights come on.

  • Service & shift flow
    Ticket timing, expo flow, prep lists, and simple “do this next” systems that people actually follow.
  • Brand & menus
    Clean menus, readable layouts, and basic web presence so guests know when you’re open and what you serve.
  • Kitchen cleaning & grease removal
    Practical deep cleaning plans: line reset, walls, hoods, floors, and degreasing sessions that bring a kitchen back from the dead.
