Hometown Flavor, Trackside Spirit

Family-friendly dining just steps from the railroad tracks. Breakfasts done right, burgers with heart, and locals who still know your name. Trackside Café keeps the tradition alive with every plate served.

Menu Highlights

Fluffy omelets, French toast, hand-pattied burgers, chili, and daily specials that taste like they came from your grandma’s kitchen — if she ran a diner with stainless steel counters and a coffee pot that never stops pouring.
