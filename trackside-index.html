Hometown Flavor, Trackside Spirit

Family-friendly dining right next to the tracks in Flushing, Michigan. Breakfast made right, burgers built with care, and coffee that never stops pouring. Trackside Café brings classic diner comfort with small-town pride.

Menu Highlights

Fluffy omelets, hearty biscuits and gravy, hand-pattied burgers, chili, and daily specials that taste like home. Check our rotating pies and desserts — they’re worth slowing down for.
